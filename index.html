A Revival Born From Rejection

Families carry stories. Some stories contain joy while others carry wounds, rejection, fear, and words spoken by others that linger long after moments pass. Through grace, belonging, holiness, and hope, a different story can rise. Revival often begins in places many overlook and among people many underestimate.
